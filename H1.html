Action FILM

Thrilling, exciting, adrenaline rush

It is a type of entertainment film, the plot of which often includes a series of action scenes, such as fighting, stunts, car chases or explosions, etc. The plot is usually a battle between good and evil, and the solution is often violence.

Die Hard
