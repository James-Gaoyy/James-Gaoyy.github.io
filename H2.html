    The collision of grand scenes and profound history

    War films (formerly known as war films) are films based on war and other military operations. They are based on real or fictional military operations. They captivate audiences with their powerful narratives and visuals, subtly influencing public perceptions of war, conveying specific political agendas, and even reshaping understandings of history. War films can serve as tools for policy advocacy, as well as being a powerful medium for criticizing war and sparking reflection.

    Representative works:

    Unsere Mütter, unsere Väter (Generation War)

    Release Year: 2013

    Director: Philipp Kadelbach

    A German World War II television series that follows five German friends from 1941 to 1945, showing how the war dramatically impacts their lives and relationships.
